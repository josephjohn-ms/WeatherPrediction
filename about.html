Welcome to WAQI

WAQI (Weather Air Quality Index) is your go-to platform for accurate weather forecasts and real-time air quality information. Our mission is to provide you with the most reliable and up-to-date data to help you make informed decisions about your daily activities based on the weather and air quality conditions.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of meteorologists, environmental scientists, and web developers passionate about delivering valuable information to our users. Our expertise lies in combining meteorological data and air quality measurements to provide a comprehensive overview of the current environmental conditions.
